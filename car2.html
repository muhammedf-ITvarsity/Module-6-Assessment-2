The Audi R8 V10 RWD – with its 5.2-litre V10 FSI engine, 397 kW of power and maximum torque of 540 Nm – accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.7 seconds in the Coupé² and takes just a tenth of a second longer in the Spyder. The R8 V10 RWD¹ is driven by the rear axle. RWD sport suspension that has been specially adapted for this concept provides a sporty and poised transfer of power to the road.
